Directions

  1. Enter Kortright Centre for Conservation in your GPS, which will bring you to the Kortright main entrance. You will see a big Kortright sign there. Punch in Buzzer Code #1103 and the gate will open.
The GPS might bring you to the side entrance instead. Only enter from the main entrance.
  1. Follow the path until you reach the main parking lot.
  1. Park your car at the parking lot and walk to the front. You will see a walking path on the left that will lead you to the visitor centre.
  1. Follow the directional signage along the way and you will reach your destination.

Drop-off/Pick-up Area
  1. Once you are the front of the parking lot, take the path as shown on the picture above (Service & Delivery Vehicle Only). That is the service road entrance.
  2. Follow the path and you will come across a 3-ways intersection. Take the path to your left.
  3. Keep going straight and you will get past the Sugar Bush Shop.
  4. The path to the left will lead you to the Glass House. The path to the right will bring you to the Cathedral of Tree and the Forest Gallery entrance.
