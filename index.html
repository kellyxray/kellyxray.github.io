Directions

  1. Enter Kortright Centre for Conservation in your GPS, which will bring you to the Kortright main entrance. You will see a big Kortright sign there. Punch in the gate code #1103 and the gate will open.
The GPS might bring you to the side entrance instead. Only enter from the main entrance.
  1. Follow the path until you reach the main parking lot.
  1. Park your car at the parking lot and walk to the front. You will see a walking path on the left that will lead you to the visitor centre.
  1. Follow the directional signage along the way and you will reach your destination.
Drop-off / Pick-up Area
  1. Once you are the front of the parking lot, take the path as shown on the picture above (Service & Delivery Vehicle Only). That is the service road entrance.
  1. Follow the path and you will come across a 3-ways intersection. Take the path to your left.
  1. Keep going straight and you will get past the Sugar Bush Shop.
  1. The path to the left will lead you to the Glass House and the Visitor Centre where Guests may be dropped off and picked up.
Important Note: The service road to the Visitor Centre is a fire route and it is for a quick drop-off / pick-up only. Parking is strictly prohibited here.

Questions

Can I bring a date or my children to the wedding?

Please kindly refer to your invitation for specific details about bringing a date or your children. The invitation will specify if additional guests are welcome.

Where should I park?

There is plenty of free parking at the venue.

Please note that entrance into the parking lot requires a gate code after 4:00 PM. The gate code is #1103.

If you plan to use a ride-hailing service for the end of the night, please provide the driver with the gate code.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Our wedding ceremony is semi-outdoors, but our cocktail hour and reception will be indoors.

Guests attending our wedding should dress warmly in layers, with jackets, scarves, and gloves recommended. Warm blankets, hand warmers, and space heaters will be provided for added comfort.

Please check the forecast closer to the date for any updates.

What should I wear?

Semi-formal attire is preferred. Men may wear a suit and tie. Women may wear an elegant dress or a sophisticated pantsuit. Kids may wear their best outfits. Everyone is encouraged to dress to impress and party.

Are there any colours that guests should avoid wearing?

We kindly ask guests to refrain from wearing white attire to our wedding.

Is it okay to take pictures with our phones and cameras during the wedding?

We kindly ask our guests to please refrain from taking photos during the ceremony.

Our professional photographer and videographer will be capturing all the special moments, and we want everyone to fully enjoy and be present in the moment. Additionally, we kindly ask that guests avoid standing in the aisle to ensure our photographer and videographer have clear access.

Throughout the night, you're more than welcome to take and share pictures. Alternatively, we have a delightful photo booth set up for you to capture your fun and shareable moments.
